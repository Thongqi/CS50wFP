Kuah Town Night Market
h

The Kuah Town Night Market in Langkawi, Malaysia, is centrally located near Dataran Lang (Eagle Square). It offers a variety of Malaysian street food, seafood, and local delicacies. Parking can be found nearby, and the market also features stalls selling handicrafts, souvenirs, clothing, and artwork. It's a bustling spot for both tourists and locals alike, open in the evenings until around 10 or 11 PM.

Day 2

Island Hopping
h

Island hopping in Langkawi is a popular activity where visitors explore the archipelago's stunning islands and attractions. It typically involves taking boat tours to visit places like Pulau Dayang Bunting (Isle of the Pregnant Maiden), known for its picturesque lake where visitors can swim and the iconic eagle feeding sessions at Pulau Singa Besar (Big Lion Island). Other stops often include Pulau Beras Basah (Wet Rice Island) for its beautiful beaches and snorkeling spots. These trips offer a chance to experience Langkawi's natural beauty, marine life, and unique landmarks.


  • Can book via online
  • Fees do not include entrance fee
  • Can swim at Dayang Lake (life jacket RM5) and kayak (around RM10)
  • Typically spend 3-4 hours
  • Price: RM35-40 per person

Underwater World Langkawi
h

Underwater World Langkawi is an ideal attraction for visitors of all ages. It features a captivating underwater tunnel where you can walk through while surrounded by marine life such as sharks, stingrays, and colorful fish. The aquarium also offers feeding sessions where you can witness marine creatures being fed, providing an educational and interactive experience. Whether you're a family with children or an adult, Underwater World Langkawi promises a memorable visit with its diverse exhibits and engaging activities.


Feeding times:

  • 2:30pm - Seals (except Fridays at 4:00pm)
  • 2:45pm - African Penguins
  • 3:00pm - Rockhopper Penguins
  • 3:30pm - Tunnel Tank
  • 4:15pm - Otter
Pantai Cenang
h

Pantai Cenang in Langkawi offers stunning sunsets that are a highlight for visitors. As the day winds down, many gather along the beachfront to witness the vibrant colors of the setting sun against the horizon.
In terms of nightlife, Pantai Cenang has a lively scene with beach bars, pubs, and restaurants that come alive after dark. You can enjoy a variety of options from relaxed beachfront lounges offering cocktails and live music to more upbeat venues with DJs and dancing. The nightlife here caters to different tastes, whether you're looking for a laid-back evening by the sea or a more energetic atmosphere to socialize and unwind.
